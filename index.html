I am interested in natural language processing. Here are some areas I have worked on:

  • Semantic parsing: Parse the input sentence into some representation of its meaning. In particular, I am interested in executable representations such as database queries or computer programs.
  • Dialog system: Understand the user's sentences within their contexts (prior utterances and the environment).
  • Web interaction: Interact with possibly unseen web pages based on the given natural language commands.

I graduated from the Computer Science Department at Stanford University, where I was advised by Percy Liang. I was part of the Stanford NLP Group. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from MIT. I like ice cream, coding, and puzzles.
