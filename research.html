Resources

  • SEMPRE: Our framework for training semantic parsers from denotations.
  • WikiTableQuestions Dataset: A dataset of complex question on Wikipedia tables.
    • Dataset viewer
    • SEMPRE models trained on the dataset.
    • DPD Outputs: Inferred consistent logical forms for the training examples in the WikiTableQuestions dataset.
  • SCONE: Sequential CONtext-dependent Execution dataset.
  • MiniWoB++ Benchmark: A benchmark for learning reinforcement learning agents that interact with websites. An extension of OpenAI MiniWoB benchmark.
